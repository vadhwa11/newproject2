OPD (Clinical Information and Specialties)

Module Overview
The OPD module helps in storing and viewing the patient visit related information. This module maintains the doctors/consultants diagnosis ,patient vital science, Excuse duty details,AFMSF-7A medical case sheet, Patient treatment plan, patient previous visit details, Patient Prescription details and Patient diagnostics details.

In this Module has following Sub Modules
  1. OPD Waiting List
  2. OPD Cardiology
  3. OPD ENT
  4. OPD Onco Surgery Case Sheet
  5. OPD Ophthalmology
  6. Ophthalmology Follow Up
  7. Retinal Laser Proforma
  8. OPD Pediatric Case Sheet
  9. OPD Urology Case Sheet
  10. OPD -Main (AFMSF-7A 2002) View
  11. OPD- Main (AFMSF- 7A 2002)
  12. In Patient Surgery Requisition Form
