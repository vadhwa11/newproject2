Married Accommodation Module For Officers & Airmen

Module Overview
Command Hospital Air Force Bangalore (CHAFB) needs to maintain the Married Accommodation SMQS automatic allotment for officers and Airmen. At any given time the Waiting list will display.

In this Module has following Sub Modules
  1. Pool Category
  2. Pool Master
  3. Location Master
  4. SMQ Master
  5. Car Garage Master
  6. Accommodation Registration for Airmen
  7. Allotment of married Accomodation for Airmen
  8. Cancellation of Allotment Order for Airmen
  9. SMQ Vacation for Airmen
  10. Allotment/Vacation Of SMQ for Airmen
  11. Occupancy / Vacation Return for Airmen
  12. Accomodation registration for Officer
  13. Issue of Willingness Certificate
  14. Allotment Of SMQs/Garages Officers
  15. Cancellation of Allotment Order for Officer
  16. SMQ Vacation for Officer
  17. Relegation Process
  18. Allotment/Vacation Of SMQ for Officer
  19. Occupancy / Vacation Return for Officer
  20. Non Availability Certificate (NAC)
