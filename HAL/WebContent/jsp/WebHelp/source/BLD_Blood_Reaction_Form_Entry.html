Blood Reaction Form Entry

Screen Overview
By using this screen user can do Blood Reaction Form Entry .Just search your data and you will get the records into grid and click on the particular record for which you need to do Blood Reaction Form Entry .






Search (Button):
User can search the records based on the HIN No.,Service No.,A&D No.,Patient First Name, Patient Last Name,Ward.
GRID (Display):
Based on search criteria data will display in the form of table. At a time only five (5) records will be displayed. To navigate or look for the next record pagination (the system of numbering pages) will be there.
OK Button and Text Box:
If user wants to switch directly to any particular page no, then he can simply enter the page no. in the given text box and click on OK Button.
Blood Reaction Form Entry Page:
This section will give the details of the record selected by you from the grid like - Entry No.,Reaction Date,Patient Blood Group,Service No.,HIN No.,Rank Nk,Patient Name, Unit Address,Service Type,Sex,Age.
