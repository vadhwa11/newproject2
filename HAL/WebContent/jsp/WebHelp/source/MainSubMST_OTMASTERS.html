OT Masters
This module deals with the full-fledged Operation Theater management system. The scope of the module covers OT Distribution entry, Surgery request by ward/OPD, , PAC (PRE-ANAESTHETIC ASSESSMENT) form entry, OT List preparation, Pre & Post Anaesthesia Procedure Notes entry, Anaesthesia register entry, Surgery procedure notes, Specimen dispatch entry, Human body parts disposal entry etc. The Ward Management module which implemented in Phase I, maintains the Drugs demand, issue/consumption & stock. Log book & Loan card is maintained in Non Expendable store module.

In this Module has following Sub Modules
  1. OT Entry
  2. OT Distribution
  3. OT Change Details
  4. Anesthesia
