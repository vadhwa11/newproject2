Blood Bank

Module Overview
Command Hospital Air Force Bangalore (CHAFB) needs to maintain the Blood Bank records, Blood stock register etc. efficiently at their hospital. At any given time the Blood bank Module should be able to get the correct stock position, receipt & issue records.

In this Module has following Sub Modules
  1. Blood Component Master
  2. Consent for Blood Transfusion Entry
  3. Blood Request Entry
  4. Pending For Blood Grouping & Cross Matching
  5. Blood Issue Entry
  6. Blood Donation Entry
  7. Update Blood Donation Entry
  8. Donor Blood Pending For Sample Screering
  9. Blood Reaction Form Entry
  10. Update Blood Reaction
  11. Investigation Pending For Transfusion Reaction
  12. Blood Test Entry
  13. Update Blood Test Entry
  14. Blood Component Separation
  15. Pending Blood Discard Entry
  16. Blood Stock Opening Balance Entry
  17. Pending for Sample Receipt Entry
  18. Pending Sample Validation
