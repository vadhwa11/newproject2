Patient Visit
Once patient get registered and wants to consult the doctor or any department of OPD, then one op visit record will be entered. It will generate the visit no of the patient. With help of visit no. we can trac the medical history of every visit of the specific patient. For every op visit, Patient visit record has to be maintained.
